Neon Salvage

Arcade survival game

Objective

Grab glowing cells before your energy drains. Every fourth pickup increases the level and sends faster drones into the arena.

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys move
  • Space activates a short shield pulse
  • P pauses the game
  • On mobile, drag anywhere on the game area

Power Cells

Standard cell: +10 score
Overcharged cell: +25 score and energy boost
Drone contact drains energy unless shielded
